How does OD Fitness Work?

Your journey with OD Fitness begins with a free 30 minute consultation call, where we will discuss your present engagement with fitness, training history, and future goals and ambitions. Programme delivery methods will be outlined, alongside a timeframe for achieving these goals.

Should you choose to pursue with OD Fitness, you will then receive an invite onto the Teambuildr suite. This platform allows for tailor made programmes to be sent directly to you, alongside explanations, videos, and messaging features. In addition to Teambuildr access, clients will receive weekly check in calls to discuss their progress, and will have access to 24/7 messaging support.
