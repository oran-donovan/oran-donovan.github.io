How Online Coaching Works:

Your journey with OD Fitness begins with a free 30-minute consultation call, where we will talk about your present engagement with fitness, training history, and future goals and ambitions. The 6-Week Programme delivery method will be outlined, and we will discusss what it means to you to achieve these goals.

When you choose to pursue with OD Fitness, you will then receive an invite to the TeamBuildr suite. This platform allows for tailor-made programmes to be sent directly to you, alongside explanations, videos, and messaging features. In addition to Teambuildr access, you will receive weekly check-in calls to discuss your progress, and will have access to 24/7 messaging support.
